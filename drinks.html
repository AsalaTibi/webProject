Refreshing Melon Cucumber Soda

Ingredients

3½ cups (542 g) chopped honeydew melon
2 limes, juiced
½ Tbsp (3 g) freshly grated ginger
2 cups (400 g) crushed ice
¼ cucumber, thinly sliced
1½ cups (360 mL) carbonated water
½ Tbsp (7 mL) agave syrup, or more to taste

Directions

1-To a blender, add the melon, lime juice, and fresh ginger. Blend until smooth.
2-To create the optional decorative rim, run a lime wedge around the rims of your serving glasses. Pour some sugar on a small plate and dip the edge of the glass in the sugar- and voilà! You've got a decorative rim!
3-Divide the crushed ice between the glasses, and top it with the cucumber slices.
4-Pour overtop two parts blended melon mixture, to one part carbonated water. Add agave syrup to taste, and enjoy!
