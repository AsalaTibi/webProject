Peanut Butter & Jelly Mug Cake

Ingredients

2 Tbsp (16 g) all-purpose flour
¼ tsp baking powder
1 pinch salt
2 Tbsp (30 mL) peanut butter
1½ Tbsp (22 mL) unsweetened soy milk
1 Tbsp (15 mL) maple syrup
½ tsp pure vanilla extract
2 Tbsp (40 g) berry chia jam

Directions

1-In a large microwavable mug, mix together the flour, baking powder, and salt.
2-Add the peanut butter, milk, maple syrup, and vanilla extract.
Mix until just combined. Be careful not to over-mix.
3-Add the jam, and gently swirl it into the batter.
4-Microwave on high (800 - 900 W) for 2 minutes, or until
a toothpick inserted into the centre comes out clean. The cooking time may vary depending on your microwave and, settings. If the cake isn't cooked through yet, simply place it back in the microwave at intervals of 15 seconds until done. Let the mug cake cool slightly, add your desired toppings, and enjoy!

Raspberry Lemonade Smoothie Bowl

Ingredients

1½ cups (225 g) frozen raspberries
1 frozen banana
1 lemon, juiced
½ cup (120 mL) unsweetened soy yogurt*
½ cup (68 g) roasted cashews
¼ cup (60 mL) unsweetened soy milk
1 Tbsp (15 mL) agave syrup, or more to taste

Directions

1-Add all ingredients to a high-speed blender and combine on high until smooth. Stop to scrape down the sides as needed. 2-Taste test. The raspberries are tart and the lemon is sour. If you feel it's needed, add more agave syrup (or maple syrup) to cut through the tartness. 3-Divide into bowls and top with fresh fruit, granola and chia seeds, if desired. Enjoy!

Refreshing Melon Cucumber Soda

Ingredients

3½ cups (542 g) chopped honeydew melon
2 limes, juiced
½ Tbsp (3 g) freshly grated ginger
2 cups (400 g) crushed ice
¼ cucumber, thinly sliced
1½ cups (360 mL) carbonated water
½ Tbsp (7 mL) agave syrup, or more to taste

Directions

1-To a blender, add the melon, lime juice, and fresh ginger. Blend until smooth.
2-To create the optional decorative rim, run a lime wedge around the rims of your serving glasses. Pour some sugar on a small plate and dip the edge of the glass in the sugar- and voilà! You've got a decorative rim!
3-Divide the crushed ice between the glasses, and top it with the cucumber slices.
4-Pour overtop two parts blended melon mixture, to one part carbonated water. Add agave syrup to taste, and enjoy!

Creamy Coleslaw with Tahini Dijon Dressing

Ingredients

Dressing

1 lime, juiced
⅓ cup (80 mL) tahini
1 Tbsp (15 mL) Dijon mustard
⅓ cup (80 mL) water
2 tsp (10 mL) apple cider vinegar
2 tsp (10 mL) agave syrup

Salad

5 cups (320 g) shredded green cabbage
2 carrots, shredded
½ daikon radish, shredded, drained (optional)
⅓ cup (48 g) roasted sunflower seeds

Directions

1-Place all of the dressing ingredients in a blender on high, stopping occasionally to scrape down the sides. Continue until creamy. Alternatively, whisk all of the ingredients together in a bowl.
2-Toss all salad ingredients and dressing in a large bowl. Ideally, allow the salad to sit for at least 15 minutes for the cabbage to soften. Serve and enjoy!
